Instant Pot Chicken Feet Bone Broth

Bone broth is becoming increasingly popular as it has been shown to have numerous health benefits. This recipe is cheap, simple, and super low-maintenance. While it may take a couple hours to complete, you don't have to do much aside from the initial prep and can either work or do some other errands while it is cooking. Talk about productivity!

Ingredients

  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 2 teaspoons sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 pound chicken feet

Directions

  1. Place the carrots, celery, onion, salt, pepper, and chicken feet in the instant pot.
  2. Pour 8 cups of water on top.
  3. Lock the lid in place, and make sure it's set to sealing.
  4. Set your Instant Pot to the soup function.
  5. Hi the + button until it reaches 120 minutes.
    Slow release for at least 10 minutes before releasing the pressure. You can also just wait for the Instant Pot to depressurize by itself.
  6. Strain the broth and pour it into storage containers.
